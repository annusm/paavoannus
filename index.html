About

I worked for over 25 years as a sound archivist at the Estonian Film Archive, focusing on preserving, digitizing, and restoring film soundtracks. I developed standards for analog and digital audio preservation and curated historical collections. Today, I focus on academic writing in the fields of media heritage, cultural memory, and archival ethics.
